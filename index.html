I am currently a Research Fellow of Transportation at the Nanyang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore, where I obtained my Ph.D. from Civil and environmental engineering in 2019 with University Research Scholarship. I also received my B.Eng. with First Class Honors at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST).

My research focuses on addressing the urban mobility issues towards the smart transport future with strategic planning and management.

我现在在新加坡南洋理工大学（NTU）土木及环境工程学系 担任博士后研究员。我亦于南洋理工大学取得博士学位，并获得学校研究奖学金。此前，我也在香港科技大学 (HKUST)获得甲等荣誉工学士学位.

我的研究主要是关于策略性的交通规划与管理，以应对面向未来智能交通时代衍生的各种城市交通问题。
